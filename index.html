第 2 页
这是什么？
这是一个YearCompass——确切地说，是你自己的YearCompass。 这是一本可以帮助你回顾过去和计划新的一年的手册。YearCompass通过捕捉细致的问题和练习，来帮助你挖掘你的生活方式，为你设计美好的一年。 这本小册子分为两个部分：第一个部分是帮助你回看前一年，从中学到，并庆祝自己过去一年的成就；第二部分是关于未来的，你会畅想和计划将来的一年，为新的一年做好准备。
我需要什么来填写这本书手册？
一只笔，不被打扰的几个小时，和一颗开放而诚实的心。
可以一个小组一起做吗？
当然可以！找一个舒适的地方，拉上你的朋友，帮他们把手册打印出来。每个人都应该填写自己的YearCompass。但你们可以在中途短暂休息时讨论这些练习，分享他们的想法和感受。 如果你这样做，请注意你们之间的边界。每个人都只应该分享他们觉得可以分享的部分。

What is this?
This is a YearCompass—to be precise, your own YearCompass. This is a booklet that helps you look back on the past year and plan for the new one. YearCompass helps you uncover your lifestyle through detailed questions and exercises, designing a wonderful year for you. This booklet is divided into two parts: The first part helps you look back on the previous year, learn from it, and celebrate your achievements; the second part is about the future, where you'll envision and plan for the coming year, preparing for the new year.
What do I need to fill out this booklet?
A pen, a few undisturbed hours, and an open and honest heart.
Can I do it with a group?
Of course! Find a comfortable place, gather your friends, and help them print out the booklet. Everyone should fill out their own YearCompass. But you can discuss these exercises during short breaks, sharing thoughts and feelings. If you do this, please respect each other's boundaries. Everyone should only share what they feel comfortable sharing.
